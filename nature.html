Valley of Flowers - Uttarakhand

Discover the breathtaking Valley of Flowers, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Located in Uttarakhand, this valley blooms with vibrant, rare flora and is surrounded by stunning Himalayan peaks, making it a paradise for nature lovers.

Kerala - Backwaters

Experience the tranquility of Kerala's backwaters. A network of serene lakes and canals, Kerala's backwaters are lined with coconut trees and traditional houseboats, offering a unique glimpse into the natural beauty of South India.

Venture into the dense forests of Meghalaya to witness the incredible living root bridges, crafted by the local Khasi tribe over generations. These natural wonders are formed by guiding the roots of rubber trees across rivers and streams.
