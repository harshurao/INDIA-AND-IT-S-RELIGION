Varanasi - Kashi Vishwanath Temple

Kashi Vishwanath Temple, located in the holy city of Varanasi, is one of the most revered temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. Situated on the banks of the Ganges, it attracts pilgrims seeking spiritual cleansing and blessings.

Amritsar - Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Harmandir Sahib, is the holiest shrine of Sikhism. Located in Amritsar, Punjab, the temple is renowned for its stunning golden architecture and the peaceful Sarovar (pool) surrounding it.

Bodh Gaya - Mahabodhi Temple

The Mahabodhi Temple in Bodh Gaya, Bihar, marks the place where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment under the Bodhi Tree. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is a pilgrimage destination for Buddhists from around the world.

Rameswaram - Ramanathaswamy Temple

Ramanathaswamy Temple, located in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the twelve Jyotirlinga temples. Known for its intricate corridors and stunning pillars, the temple holds great significance in Hindu mythology.
