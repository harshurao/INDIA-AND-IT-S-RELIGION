Taj Mahal - Agra

The Taj Mahal, an iconic symbol of love and one of the Seven Wonders of the World, is located in Agra. Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, this marble mausoleum stands as a masterpiece of Mughal architecture.

Jaipur - Amer Fort

Amer Fort, located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, is known for its grandeur and exquisite blend of Rajput and Mughal architecture. The fort's majestic halls, beautiful gardens, and intricate mirror work attract visitors from all over the world.

Konark - Sun Temple

The Sun Temple at Konark, Odisha, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a stunning example of Kalinga architecture. Built in the 13th century, the temple is dedicated to the Sun God and is designed to resemble his chariot, with intricate carvings covering its walls.

Hampi - Vijayanagara Empire

Hampi, in Karnataka, is the ancient capital of the Vijayanagara Empire and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Known for its impressive ruins, the site showcases magnificent temples, royal complexes, and the distinct Dravidian architecture.
