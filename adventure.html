Rishikesh - River Rafting

Experience the thrill of river rafting in Rishikesh, the adventure capital of India. Nestled in the Himalayas, Rishikesh offers an exhilarating rafting experience on the Ganges River, surrounded by stunning landscapes.

Leh Ladakh - Biking

Embark on an unforgettable bike ride through the rugged terrains of Leh-Ladakh. Known for its high-altitude landscapes, Ladakh offers challenging routes and mesmerizing views of the Himalayas.

Andaman Islands - Scuba Diving

Dive into the crystal-clear waters of the Andaman Islands to explore vibrant coral reefs and marine life. Andaman is a top destination for scuba diving enthusiasts looking for underwater adventures.

Manali - Paragliding

Soar through the skies in Manali, a popular destination for paragliding. With its stunning valleys and snow-capped mountains, Manali offers breathtaking views and an adrenaline-pumping experience.
